Life Insurance

Secure your family's future with our transparent and reliable life insurance policies.

Term Insurance (Pure Protection Plan)

Pure protection with no maturity benefit if you survive the policy term.

  • Affordable premiums for high coverage
  • No money back if you survive the policy term
  • Perfect for those seeking pure protection
  • Similar to car insurance concept
  • Best for young families with limited budget
  • Policy term: 1 year (renewable annually)
  • Flexible renewal options

Endowment Plans / Money-Back Plans

Combination of insurance and savings with regular payouts during the policy term.

  • Get maturity benefit if you survive the policy term
  • Acts as both insurance and savings
  • Higher premiums compared to term insurance
  • Regular payouts during policy term
  • Ideal for long-term financial planning
  • Fixed policy term: 20 years
  • Long-term commitment with guaranteed returns
